Some visual Projects

Nice Guy

Digital drawing made sometime in the fall of 2018. Or maybe it was 2017. It's hard to see at the picture's current size, but it was drawn in a pixel art style, which is why some of the edges and curves are not smooth.

Tooth Ache

Digital drawing made sometime in 2018. I wasn't going to add any more about this project, but oddly enough, if I don't add extra lines of text here, the picture becomes smaller. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Gross Strawberry

Digitally altered picture of a rotting strawberry made sometime in 2018, or possibly 2017.

Moody Acorn

Clay sculpture made in October 2019. Black-out poetry and segments of story rough drafts are glued to the exterior, and the 'cap' was colored with woodstain, coconut oil, and dishsoap.
